# Planora

Planora is a voice-first smart planner designed to make scheduling effortless. Instead of manually entering tasks, users can simply speak naturally, and the app intelligently converts their input into structured, time-based tasks.

By combining real-time speech recognition with lightweight natural language parsing, Planora understands phrases like “Meditate at 6 for 30 minutes” or “Study at 8,” automatically assigning start times and durations. This reduces friction in planning and allows users to focus on what matters instead of how to organize it.

The app emphasizes simplicity and clarity through a clean, minimal interface inspired by Apple’s design principles. Tasks are presented in an intuitive format with clear time ranges, making it easy to visualize and manage the day.

Planora is not just a to-do list—it’s a step toward more natural, human-centric productivity tools where planning feels as easy as speaking.
